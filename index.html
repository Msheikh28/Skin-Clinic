Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our treatments and clinic.

Are the treatments safe for all skin types?

Yes, all our treatments are conducted after a thorough skin analysis. We customize every procedure according to your specific skin type and medical history to ensure maximum safety and efficacy.

How many sessions will I need?

The number of sessions depends on the treatment and the severity of the concern. During your initial consultation, Dr. Ashiya will provide a personalized treatment plan with an estimated timeline.

Is there any downtime after procedures?

Most of our treatments have minimal to no downtime. Some procedures like microneedling might cause mild redness for 24-48 hours, which Dr. Ashiya will discuss with you beforehand.
